FULL UNIVERSE NOTES

US:
- Bundled with a current Nasdaq symbol directory snapshot.
- Daily refresh workflow rebuilds the US universe from Nasdaq.

IHSG:
- Bundled with full IDX CSV universe (~951 tickers).
- Daily refresh workflow rebuilds from maintained IDX CSV source.

CRYPTO:
- Live refresh tries Binance spot USDT first.
- If Binance is blocked/unavailable, refresh falls back to Coinbase USD spot products.
- If Coinbase also fails, refresh falls back to Kraken USD tradable pairs.
- If all live sources fail, the app keeps the last saved crypto universe instead of dropping back to a tiny starter list.

Why the app can look empty on first deploy:
- The app is snapshot-first and does not fetch heavy live data on page load.
- The GitHub Action is responsible for building refreshed universes, histories, and snapshots.
